Who Are We?

The "Bad Students" movement in Thailand is a student-led
social and political movement that emerged in 2020, calling
for reforms in the Thai education system and democracy in
the country. The movement seeks to address a wide range
of issues in the Thai education system, including the lack of
academic freedom, the high cost of education, and the
limited opportunities for critical thinking and creativity. The
movement also advocates for greater political reform and
transparency in government, calling for a new constitution,
an end to military influence in politics, and the protection
of human rights.

Despite facing obstacles and repression, the "Bad Students"
movement has continued to gain momentum and has
inspired other social and political movements in Thailand.
Through their activism and protests, these students have
become a symbol of hope and change for many people in
Thailand, demonstrating the power of youth-led movements
in shaping the future of their country.
